About Us

Built on nearly two decades of experience

Founded in 2007, National UC Realty (NUCR) specializes in Site Selection and Site Analysis for medical clinics nationally and has taken part in over 600 medical real estate transactions.

By utilizing a proprietary, in-depth site scoring algorithm that analyzes demographics, psychographics, medical competition, ACS Insurance data and other key metrics we’re able to increase confidence and decrease the time required to identify prime locations.

Working with us is like having the nation’s best in-house medical site selection staff, without any of the overhead!
